Pizza Diavola Recipe

Home

Description

Pizza Diavola is a spicy pizza that is a favorite among many. It features a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and spicy salami. The heat from the salami combined with the creamy mozzarella creates a delicious contrast that is sure to please any pizza lover. Here is what you'll need to make it

Ingredients

  • 5oz / 150ml San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Parmesan
  • 3oz / 60g Fior Di Latte Mozzarella
  • 6 Slices of Spicy Salami
  • 1 Meaty Pork Sausage
  • 1 Handful of Kalamata Olives
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 2 tsp Chili Flakes
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • olive oil

Directions

  1. Spread the tomatoes on the pizza to form a base
  2. Break the spicy salami into pieces on the pizza
  3. Remove the pork sausage from the case and crumble the meat over the pizza
  4. Add chili flakes to taste. (If you prefer less spicy, skip the flakes)
  5. Add the black olives and sprinkle with olive oil
  6. Add the mozzarella and sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  7. Bake to perfection in the Revolve for 70 seconds.
